Some common questions
were often asked

Can you use spray paint with stencils?

We would not recommend using spray paints with stencils because spray paints are not viscous enough to get sharp imprints.

Which Is the Best Colour for a False Ceiling Colour Combination for a Luxurious Look?

For a luxurious look, opt for rich and regal hues such as gold, deep burgundy, or royal blue. These colours instantly elevate the ambiance of a room, creating an opulent and sophisticated atmosphere

How Can I Use Inexpensive Materials for Luxurious-Looking Pop Ceiling Colour Combinations?

Achieve a luxurious look on a budget by using affordable materials like PVC panels or lightweight gypsum boards. These materials can be easily painted and moulded into various shapes, allowing you to create intricate designs without breaking the bank.

Why Is Plaster of Paris the Preferred Material for False Ceiling Colour Combinations?

Plaster of Paris (POP) is favoured for false ceiling installations due to its versatility, durability, and ease of customization. It can be moulded into intricate designs and painted in a variety of colours, making it an ideal choice for creating stunning ceiling combinations.
